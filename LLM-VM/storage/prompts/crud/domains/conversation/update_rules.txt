====================
Update Types
====================
- **Correction Update**: Fix factual errors in an observation's text while preserving the source reference
  - Example: "Change the guinea pig observation to say two guinea pigs" → only change text, keep source_dia_id
- **Merge Update**: When new information supplements an existing observation, merge into a single updated text
  - Example: "Update the adoption observation to include that she passed the interview" → extend text with new detail

====================
Field Preservation Rules
====================
- source_dia_id: NEVER modify this field — it is the ground-truth link to the original dialogue turn
- text: May be modified for corrections or merges
- text: May be modified for corrections or merges
