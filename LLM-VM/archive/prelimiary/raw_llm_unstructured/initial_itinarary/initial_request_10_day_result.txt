**Day 1: Welcome to Toronto – Urban Exploration & Local Delicacies**

1. **St. Lawrence Market (Breakfast)**
   - *Time Block:* 8:00 AM - 9:30 AM
   - *Description:* Kickstart your Toronto adventure with a hearty breakfast at the iconic St. Lawrence Market. Explore countless stalls offering everything from fresh bagels to a classic peameal bacon sandwich.
   - *Travel Method:* Walk
   - *Expected Cost:* $10-$20
   - *Highlights:* Historical charm, diverse food stalls, local treats.

2. **Distillery District**
   - *Time Block:* 9:45 AM - 11:30 AM
   - *Description:* Stroll through Toronto's Distillery District, a pedestrian-only area filled with Victorian architecture, boutiques, and art galleries.
   - *Travel Method:* Walk
   - *Expected Cost:* Free
   - *Highlights:* Cobblestone streets, contemporary art galleries, craft shops.

3. **CN Tower (Lunch with a View)**
   - *Time Block:* 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM
   - *Description:* Enjoy lunch at the revolving 360 Restaurant atop the CN Tower, featuring breathtaking views of the city.
   - *Travel Method:* Streetcar
   - *Expected Cost:* $60-$100
   - *Highlights:* Panoramic city views, classic Canadian dishes.

4. **Ripley's Aquarium of Canada**
   - *Time Block:* 2:15 PM - 4:00 PM
   - *Description:* Dive into an underwater world at Ripley's Aquarium, located just steps away from the CN Tower, showcasing diverse marine life.
   - *Travel Method:* Walk
   - *Expected Cost:* $30-$40
   - *Highlights:* Massive shark tank, colorful jellyfish, interactive exhibits.

5. **Eaton Centre (Shopping)**
   - *Time Block:* 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM
   - *Description:* Head to Eaton Centre, Toronto's largest shopping mall, offering a wide array of stores to suit every taste.
   - *Travel Method:* Subway
   - *Expected Cost:* Variable
   - *Highlights:* Architecturally impressive glass dome, large selection of retailers.

6. **King Street West (Dinner)**
   - *Time Block:* 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM
   - *Description:* Enjoy a dinner on King Street West, known for its eclectic mix of restaurants. Try Buca for Italian-inspired cuisine.
   - *Travel Method:* Streetcar
   - *Expected Cost:* $50-$70
   - *Highlights:* Modern atmosphere, creative dishes, excellent service.

7. **Rex Jazz & Blues Bar**
   - *Time Block:* 8:30 PM - 10:30 PM
   - *Description:* Conclude your first day at Rex Jazz & Blues Bar, a staple in the Toronto music scene, featuring live performances.
   - *Travel Method:* Walk
   - *Expected Cost:* $15-$25
   - *Highlights:* Intimate setting, great acoustics, talented musicians.
