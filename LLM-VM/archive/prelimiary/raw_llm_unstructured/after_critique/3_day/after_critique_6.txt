## Revised Itinerary:

**Day 2: Cultural and Scenic Toronto**

1. **Kensington Market for Breakfast at Pow Wow Cafe**
   - **Time Block:** 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
   - **Description:** Start the day with unique Indigenous-inspired brunch dishes like Bannock tacos.
   - **Travel Method:** Public Transit (TTC - 10 minutes)
   - **Expected Cost:** CAD 15-20
   - **Notable Highlights:** Bohemian vibes, eclectic shops nearby.

2. **Royal Ontario Museum (ROM)**
   - **Time Block:** 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM
   - **Description:** Discover an immense collection of art, culture, and natural history exhibits.
   - **Travel Method:** Walk (15 minutes)
   - **Expected Cost:** CAD 23
   - **Notable Highlights:** Dinosaur gallery, Chinese architecture exhibit, Bloor street architecture.

3. **Lunch at Trattoria Nervosa**
   - **Time Block:** 1:30 PM - 2:30 PM
   - **Description:** Delight in classic Italian dishes at this cozy trattoria, offering a charming setting perfect for a midday break.
   - **Travel Method:** Walk (5 minutes)
   - **Expected Cost:** CAD 30-40
   - **Notable Highlights:** House-made pasta, warm ambiance.

4. **Casa Loma**
   - **Time Block:** 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
   - **Description:** Explore Toronto’s majestic castle, complete with secret passages, unique rooms, and lush gardens.
   - **Travel Method:** Public Transit (TTC - 20 minutes)
   - **Expected Cost:** CAD 30
   - **Notable Highlights:** Dramatic architecture, panoramic city views.

5. **Dinner at DaiLo**
   - **Time Block:** 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM
   - **Description:** Enjoy Asian fusion cuisine with a modern Canadian twist at this top-rated restaurant.
   - **Travel Method:** Public Transit (TTC - 20 minutes)
   - **Expected Cost:** CAD 50-60
   - **Notable Highlights:** Creative dishes, trendy setting.

6. **Rivoli for Live Music**
   - **Time Block:** 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM
   - **Description:** Experience a variety of live music performances in this iconic venue, which often features up-and-coming musicians from jazz to indie pop.
   - **Travel Method:** Walk (10 minutes)
   - **Expected Cost:** CAD 10-20
   - **Notable Highlights:** Historic ambiance, blend of musical genres.
